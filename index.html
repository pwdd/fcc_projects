About

I'm a web developer. But I wasn't.

I was a journalist and had a successful career. I worked as an Editor of culture and arts sections and my last job was in one of the most important magazines in São Paulo, Brazil.

Then I quit.

It's not that I had the money to stop working. I just couldn't do that anymore.

It was time to do something that I wanted since I was a kid: learn how to program. It was love at first 'hello world'. The amount of fun I have while coding and the rewarding feeling when the job is done are what I missed.

At that time, I was also in love with an American. We came to spend some time in the U.S. and decided to stay.

Letting everything behind was hard. Family, friends, flavors, more than one thousand CDs and over five hundred books. But it was worth it.
